## **How to Send This Email:**

### **Option 1: Gmail Scheduled Send**
1. Open Gmail → Compose new email
2. Copy the above text
3. Click the down arrow → **Schedule send**
4. Choose date & time (morning of her birthday)
5. Send

### **Option 2: Outlook Scheduled Send**
1. Open Outlook → New email
2. Paste the template
3. Click **Delay Delivery**
4. Set date & time
5. Send

### **Option 3: Email Scheduler Services**
- **Mailchimp** (Free tier)
- **Sendinblue** 
- **Right Inbox**
- **Boomerang for Gmail**
